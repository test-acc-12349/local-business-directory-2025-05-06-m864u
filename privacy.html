Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 14, 2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to Local Business Directory. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our directory website and services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Directory Listings

  • Business name and contact information
  • Business description and categories
  • Operating hours and location
  • Photos and media content
  • Reviews and ratings

2.2 User Information

  • Name and email address
  • Account credentials
  • Usage data and preferences
  • IP address and device information

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To maintain and improve our directory services
  • To communicate with users about their listings
  • To process and display reviews
  • To prevent fraud and abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our website and hold certain information. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

5. Your Rights

Under GDPR and CCPA, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Data portability

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: contact@directory.com
